About Us More About Us

Building Futures,
Not Just Structures

Dixon offers comprehensive services designed to allow the company to do whatever it takes to finish a project. Some of these services include; design work, remodeling and alterations, deck construction, site preparation, carpentry, fence installation, concrete foundations, driveways, sidewalks, flatwork, painting, utilities installation, and furnishing and installing structural elements. In addition, skills Dixon lacks can be subcontracted.

Services our Service

High Quality Services

Pre-Construction

We collaborate with clients early to review drawings, create trade estimates, and suggest value engineering for budget friendly, efficient project execution.

Project Management

We analyze specs and drawings, provide cost saving value engineering, and liaise with contractors, ensuring compliance and smart decisions throughout the project.

Construction Management

Our managers oversee day-to-day site work, ensure on time, on budget delivery, maintain clear communication, and offer cost saving solutions using deep industry knowledge.

Design-Build

From concept to completion, we handle full design-build services, managing consultants and trades to deliver customized, efficient, turn key industrial or retail spaces.

General Contracting

Dedicated managers visit sites, review progress, meet with contractors, and keep owners informed with regular reports and resolutions to any issues or delays.

Site Management

We take full control of construction handling permits, labor, materials, and schedules to deliver high quality results with minimal client involvement.
